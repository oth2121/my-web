📖 Crypto Glossary

HODL

A misspelling of "hold" that became a rallying cry in the crypto community, meaning to hold onto your assets despite price volatility.

FOMO

Fear Of Missing Out. The feeling that you need to buy a certain asset because its price is rapidly increasing, and you don't want to miss potential gains.

Degen

Short for "degenerate," often used affectionately to describe investors who take high risks in pursuit of high rewards, particularly in DeFi or memecoins.

DYOR

Do Your Own Research. A critical piece of advice in crypto, encouraging investors to thoroughly investigate projects before committing funds.

Ape In

To invest heavily and often impulsively into a cryptocurrency project, typically a high-risk memecoin or NFT, driven by excitement and community hype.

Wen Lambo?

A common phrase used in crypto communities to express anticipation for a cryptocurrency's price to rise enough to afford luxury items like a Lamborghini.
